## ⚙️ Features Overview

keyfi makes group decision-making easier by providing these tools:

- **Submit**: Enter your suggestions or ideas.  
- **Vote**: Choose options that you or your group prefer.  
- **Unlock**: View the final results once voting ends.  
- **Collaborate**: Share your project with friends or team members.

The app works offline for basic functions and syncs online when connected.

## 🌐 How keyfi Works

The app collects inputs from different users in a project.

Each user submits one or more ideas.

Then, users cast votes on submitted ideas.

When voting closes, keyfi counts the votes and shows the winning ideas.

This process helps groups pick the best choices fairly and quickly.
